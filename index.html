Giving to QE

Bringing up and instruction of boys and youth, to be brought up in grammar and other learning, and the same to continue for ever, and the said School for one Master and one Usher for ever to continue and remain and that there shall be for ever four-and-twenty discreet, honest governors.

Members of the 1573 Society give a regular gift via direct debit. We are keen for Society members to be recognised by having their name handwritten in calligraphy in the Queen Elizabeth's School Endowment Book – which they will also be offered the opportunity to sign and will be on display in the school Archive. Each year, members will be invited to the Master’s events and an annual Benefactors Garden Party, in addition to receiving a special lapel pin.

Our new Music Hall is the current focus of our Capital Projects Fund. Explore the possibility of naming a practice room or inclusion on our donors wall.
