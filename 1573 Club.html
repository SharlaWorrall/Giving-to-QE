Bringing up and instruction of boys and youth, to be brought up in grammar and other learning, and the same to continue for ever, and the said School for one Master and one Usher for ever to continue and remain and that there shall be for ever four-and-twenty discreet, honest governors.

To join the 1573 Society, you may choose to give:

  • Monthly – £104.87 (with Gift Aid) or £131.09 (without)
  • Quarterly – £314.6 (with Gift Aid) or £393.25 (without)
  • Annually – £1,258.4 (with Gift Aid) or £1,573 (without)
  • A gift of £50,000 would secure life membership to the Society.

Society members will be recognised by having their name handwritten in calligraphy in the Queen Elizabeth's School Endowment Book. They will also be offered the opportunity to sign the book which will be on display in the school Archive. Each year, members will be invited to an annual Benefactors Garden Party, in addition to receiving a special lapel pin. To join the 1573 Society, please download and return the form below, or contact the Development Office. Members of the 1573 Society give a regular gift via direct debit.
